# Reef Loretto

# "Website"

Reef Loretto is an Arab - American music producer based in Brooklyn, New York.He
grew up in Dhahran, Saudi Arabia on an Aramco residential compound near his
mother’s birthplace of Dammam.A unifying theme of his work relates to feeling
forever “out of place” in environments and the pursuit of a secure sense of
home.
